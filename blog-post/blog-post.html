    • Top 5 attractions in Dubai

    Posted: 9/2/2023 | September 2, 2023

    Dubai is the most populated city in the United Arab Emirates. It's located on the southeast coast of the Persian Gulf. Dubai is known for its wealth and being a popular luxury travel destination. Not to mention, its also known for its warm temeperatue, sandy beaches, luxury resorts, and cultural and sporting attractions. In this article, we're going to talk about the top 5 most popular attractions in Dubai.

    1. The Dubai Fountain

      2.

      The Dubai Fountain is a choreographed gountain system located on the 30-acre Burj Khalifa Lake in Dubai. It opened in May 2009. The fountain is the world's tallest performing fountain, shooting water up to 500 ft in the air. The fountain performs to a variety of music, including classical, contemporary Arabic, and world music. The fountain is free to watch from anywhere on the lake or nearby areas.

    2. Burj Khalifa

      3.

      The Burj Khalifa is the world's tallest building, standin at 2717 ft tall. It has 163 floors and includes a hotel, office space, restaurants, observation decks, and health facilities. It also has 900 residences, including studios and apartments with one to four bedrooms.

    3. The Dubai Mall

      4.

      The Dubai Mall is the world's largest mall by total land area. It opened in November 2008 and is located next to the Burj Khalifa. The mall is free to enter and has over 1,200 stores. It also has a large walk-through aquarium, an ice rink, and 14,000 parking spaces. The Dubai Mall is known for its shopping festival and has over 75 million visitors each year.

    4. Atlantis Aquaventure Waterpark

      5.

      Atlantis Aquaventure Waterpark is the world's largest waterpark. It opened in 2008. It has over 105 slides, attractions, and experiences. The park includes zip lines, slides through shark-filled lagoons, and a splash area for kids. It also has an aquarium. Due to popularity, it's recommended to buy tickets in advance.

    5. Burj Al Arab

      6.

      The Burj Al Arab is a 7-star hotel. It's one of the tallest hotels in the world and stands on an artificial island. The hotel has 56 floors and opened in December 1999. It's known for its luxurious amenities and amazing architecture. The Burj Al Arab is a private resort and is closed to non-guests. However, you can visit if you have a dining reservation at one of the restaurants. The Burj Al Arab has a variety of fine-dining restaurants and incredible beaches.
